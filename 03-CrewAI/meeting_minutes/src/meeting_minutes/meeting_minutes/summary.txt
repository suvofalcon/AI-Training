FinTech Plus reported a stellar Q2 2023 with a revenue of $125 million, a 25% year-over-year increase. The gross profit margin is 58%, while EBITDA surged to $37.5 million with a 30% margin. Net income increased to $16 million from $10 million in Q2 2022. The company has expanded its total addressable market and diversified its asset-backed securities portfolio. Their total assets are $1.5 billion with liabilities at $900 million. They noted a strong user growth, and a LTVCAC ratio of 3.5x. Risk management includes a value-at-risk model with 99% confidence. The outlook for Q3 is positive with expected revenue of $135 million and the IPO of FinTech subsidiary Pay Plus projected to raise $200 million.
